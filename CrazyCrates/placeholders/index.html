    随机数占位符:

    可用于: 给予奖励的选项中使用.

    如何使用: %random%:Minimum-Maximum

    例子

    # 这里采用的是 eco 命令 | 就是给予玩家 1w~10w 之间的随机数的钱
Commands:
- 'eco give %player% %random%:10000-100000'

    其他插件的占位符

    支持插件：PlaceholderAPI

    占位符 例子
    %crazycrates_<crate>% %crazycrates_galactic%
    %crazycrates_<crate>_physical% %crazycrates_galactic_physical%
    %crazycrates_<crate>_total% %crazycrates_galactic_total%

    支持插件：MVdWPlaceholderAPI

    占位符 例子
    {crazycrates_<crate>} {crazycrates_galactic}
    {crazycrates_<crate>_physical} {crazycrates_galactic_physical}
    {crazycrates_<crate>_total} {crazycrates_galactic_total}

    需要插件：MVdWPlaceholderAPI
